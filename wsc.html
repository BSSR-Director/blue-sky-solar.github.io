WORLD SOLAR CHALLENGE
Journey across over 3,000 kilometres of the Australian outback.
The World Solar Challenge, hosted October 22-29, 2023 in this cycle, is the team's next endeavour. Our latest solar car, Borealis, will be traversing over 3000 kilometres starting from Darwin, Australia to the finish line in Adelaide, Australia. At the race, our team will be pushing the boundaries of technological innovation as we compete in the Australian outback under harsh conditions. Have any questions?
